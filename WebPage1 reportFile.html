About School

Nathimal Ram Sahai Public School was commissioned in the year of 1977 with the joint effort of Late Shri D.S.Govil, Secretary N.R.E.C. Association and Trustees of N.R.E.C. College. Association established by R.B. Nathimal ji and Ram Sahai ji (two brothers) which runs many institutions in Khurja city. It elects a managing committee after every three years and the Manager of the school with other members look after the school. The powers of day to day Administration are rested in the Principal. The committee is constituted in accordance with the requirement of the society registration Act 1816 and laws of C.B.S.E., New Delhi.The school is at present a land mark in the field of education in our District due to sincere and dedicated efforts and co-operation of Staff and Teachers.

Sports:

The NR Public school places great emphasis on health and physical education and has a host of facilities to deliver a comprehensive PE program. Junior school students have PE 5 days a week and seniors have sport and fitness 4 days a week.There are school teams in all disciplines and training continues through the year both before and after school.Physical Education at NRPS is designed to encourage the natural interest of most children in developing their own various physical skills.
We seek to provide enjoyment through participation in a physical sport and activity, and experience the stimulation of competition. Every effort is made to ensure that children understand and are aware of the essential part that exercise plays in maintaining a healthy body, and to develop in children a determination to overcome the difficulties of mastering specific skills and movements. Children are given the opportunity to experience a variety of physical educational opportunities within the school hall, playground and sports field.

Music&Dance:-

  1. The Performing Arts Faculty comes together with Dance, Drama and Music forming an integral part of our curriculum.
  2. The dance curriculum embraces movement, choreography as well as an understanding and appreciation of folk, classical and contemporary dance.
  3. Dance rooms at junior and senior school provide creative spaces that resound with strains of folk and the bols of Kathak and Bharatanatyam.
  4. Senior students have a dance ensemble that delights everyone with their amazing performances.
  5. Students also share their performances at Special Assemblies as well as Form Mornings and Evenings which ensure that every child in the school participates.
